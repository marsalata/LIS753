Breakfast.

Skip to Yogurt Skip to Scrambled Eggs

DO NOT forget to eat breakfast. It is a brief moment of peace before you find out what fresh hell awaits you. So eat up, and enjoy. Have a coffee with it, perhaps. Add some Jameson to that coffee, if it is already one of those days. You'll figure it out. I can't help you with figuring it out, but you've probably had thousands of mornings by now, so you'll get the hang of it. But eat breakfast.

YOGURT

  • Yogurt
  • Granola
  • Fresh Fruit
Put yogurt in a bowl. Add desired amount of granola. Add fresh fruit, based on choice or availability. Procure spoon. Eat.

SCRAMBLED EGGS

  • Eggs
  • Salsa
  • Cheese
Preheat a skillet over medium-medium high heat. Crack eggs into a bowl, and whip them with a fork until frothy. Once skillet is hot, pour eggs into skillet and push them around slowly until cooked to desired doneness. Top with cheese, and mix with eggs in skillet. Transfer to plate, top with salsa of your choosing, and consume.
